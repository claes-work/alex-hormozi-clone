## Physique & build

- **Muscular, bodybuilder build.** A former competitive bodybuilder's physique carried into
  his 30s: broad, heavily developed, visibly lean. (self-reported,
  [[../wiki/sources/2024-11-18-yt-7qy-EPc2gYU]])
- **Bodyweight ~235 lb, natural.** He was **235 lb natural** in his heaviest lifting years and
  identifies as a natural (drug-free) lifter. (self-reported,
  [[../wiki/sources/2024-11-18-yt-7qy-EPc2gYU]])
- **Signature calves.** His most developed and self-identified signature body part. He trained
  calves **every single day for 10+ years**, and once gave clients calf DOMS so severe they
  could barely walk / couldn't wear heels. At peak he loaded up to a **100 lb plate on top** of
  his preferred calf machine. (self-reported, [[../wiki/sources/2024-11-18-yt-7qy-EPc2gYU]])
- **Strength markers** (peak lifts, self-reported recall, [[../wiki/sources/2024-11-18-yt-7qy-EPc2gYU]]):
  - **Deadlift:** 465 lb for 20 reps (has video); added ~160 lb to his 20-rep via a high-rep
    Juggernaut cycle.
  - **Bench (his best natural lift):** 415 lb one-rep max (paused / powerlifting-style); "every
    time I touch the bench it blows up."
  - **Squat:** ~405–455 lb for 20 reps (3 sets of 20 on barbell back squats).
  - **Walking lunges:** 225 lb "when I was strong and young."
- **Current wear-and-tear.** Now in more pain than in his volume-piling youth despite lighter
  loading; **has not trained calves in ~a year** (Nov 2024) due to a foot problem. (self-reported,
  [[../wiki/sources/2024-11-18-yt-7qy-EPc2gYU]])
