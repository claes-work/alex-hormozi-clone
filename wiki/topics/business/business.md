# business - Domain Hub

Offers, leads, sales, scaling, money models, pricing, retention.

## Pages

### Money Models (from [[../../sources/2025-08-16-100m-money-models|$100M Money Models]], 2025)

- [[money-model|Money Model]] — the umbrella framework: a deliberate sequence of offers;
  three stages (Get Cash / Get More Cash / Get The Most Cash), the benchmark ladder, build
  order, assembly rules, and the Gym Launch case study.
- [[attraction-offers|Attraction Offers]] — Stage I: turn strangers into customers (Win
  Your Money Back, Giveaways, Decoy, Buy X Get Y Free, Pay Less Now or Pay More Later).
- [[upsell-offers|Upsell Offers]] — Stage II: get customers to spend more (Classic, Menu,
  Anchor, Rollover).
- [[downsell-offers|Downsell Offers]] — Stage II: turn "no" into "yes" (Payment Plans,
  Trial With Penalty, Feature Downsells).
- [[continuity-offers|Continuity Offers]] — Stage III: recurring revenue (Bonus, Discount,
  Waived Fee).
