- [[attraction-offers|Attraction Offers]] — Stage I: turn strangers into customers (Win
  Your Money Back, Giveaways, Decoy, Buy X Get Y Free, Pay Less Now or Pay More Later).
- [[upsell-offers|Upsell Offers]] — Stage II: get customers to spend more (Classic, Menu,
  Anchor, Rollover).

  Waived Fee).
