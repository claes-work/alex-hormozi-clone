# Hormozi Writing Guidelines

Alex Hormozi's codified writing method, published as "Alex Hormozi Writing Guideliness"
[sic] in the [[../../sources/2025-08-16-affiliate-black-book|Affiliate Black Book]]
(2025, PDF p. 17–18). This is the closest thing to an explicit spec of his prose voice —
the rules his team must follow to write "as him". Direct input to `persona/voice.md`.

## The eight rules (with the book's own examples)

1. **Keep everything below 3rd-grade reading level.**
   ❌ "We utilize leverage to gain higher returns on our equity checks"
   ✅ "We borrow money so we can buy bigger stuff (and make more) than we could with our cash alone."
2. **Present voice.**
   ❌ "When you're creating your sales page" → ✅ "When you create your sales page"
3. **Active, not passive.**
   ❌ "The body was carried out of the room" → ✅ "We carried the body out of the room"
4. **Avoid adverbs whenever possible** — "adverbs usually mean lame verbs". Ban-list:
   "very, super, way, actually".
   ❌ "We shut the door really hard" → ✅ "We slammed the door"
5. **Simple/short sentences.** Usually one comma. Two commas → cut the middle or make
   two sentences.
   ❌ "We tried to escape but the man, with the red mask, chased us until we lost our breath."
   ✅ "The red-masked man chased us until we lost our breath. We were trapped."
6. **Positive language.**
   ❌ "Don't Stop" → ✅ "Keep Going"; ❌ "Don't leave the facility" → ✅ "Stay inside"
7. **Remove redundant words.**
   ❌ "We simultaneously left the building at the same time"
8. **Remove unnecessary words** (words that don't change or add meaning).
   ❌ "8 Guidelines For Writing I Live By" → ✅ "8 Writing Guidelines I Live By"
   ❌ "He was able to get out of his car." → ✅ "He exited his car."

## Why this matters for the clone

These rules are *testable*: any text the persona produces can be linted against them
(reading level, adverb count, comma count, passive constructions, negations). They also
explain the prose texture of the books themselves — see
[[../../sources/2025-08-16-100m-money-models|$100M Money Models]], which follows all
eight rules on nearly every page.
