# United Fitness

> **Stub.** Alex Hormozi's early gym brand, predating [[gym-launch|Gym Launch]]. All facts
> here are **self-reported** by Alex on camera and **not registry-verified**. Disambiguate
> from unrelated same-name fitness businesses.

United Fitness is the brand name Alex Hormozi used for his first gym(s) (~2013–2016, Southern
California / Huntington Beach area) before he pivoted into the gym-turnaround and licensing
businesses that became [[gym-launch|Gym Launch]].

## What the wiki knows (self-reported)

- On archival footage he identifies himself as **"the owner of United Fitness"**
  ([[../sources/2026-04-09-yt-Q8xXSMe8E4Q]], reacting in 2026 to his ~10-year-old content).
- Offer/operations seen in that early content: a **free 6-week challenge** with a motivation/
  screening step, **color-coded nutrition plans**, and **impedance body-fat tracking**.
- This gym is the same first-gym operation whose scaling figures appear in the biography
  (~$5k/mo → **~$35k/mo peak**, fully outsourced by month 9, second location by month 15) —
  [[../sources/2026-03-12-yt-3T7k-zVKOvg]].
- His earliest self-shot content (a "mind game series," commute/car vlogs, a DC-area campaign)
  was made under / around this era — [[../sources/2026-04-09-yt-Q8xXSMe8E4Q]].
- The free **"launch other people's gyms and split the upside"** service that "sprouted the
  entire new business" (→ Gym Launch) grew out of this period (~2015).
