# Gym Launch

Hormozi's gym-turnaround/licensing company — the business that made him rich and the
central case study of [[../sources/2025-08-16-100m-money-models|$100M Money Models]].
All facts below are self-reported in that book.

## Facts

- Origin: after selling/closing his six gyms, he flew around the US doing 30+ in-person
  gym turnarounds over two years, then switched to a licensing model — gym owners run
  his playbook themselves (PDF pp. 20–21, 192).
- The underlying gym model: Free 6 Week Challenge ads at ~$5/lead, ~20 leads/day, 50%
  show, 50% close into a $600 program (+$80 supplement profit) — "$1 in becomes $34 out
  in 48 hours"; $600 credited toward annual membership converted two-thirds →
  $20,000/month gyms within 30 days for $3,000 down (PDF pp. 19–20).
- Money model evolution and revenue milestones: Decoy Offer (free DIY vs. $16,000/16-week
  done-with-you) → $476K/mo in 3 months; + "Gym Lords" Classic Upsell ($42,000/yr,
  $36,000 prepaid via $6,000 prepay discount) with payment-plan downsell ladder →
  ~$1.5M/mo; + Menu Upsells/Feature Downsells (minimum package $100/wk) → $2.3M/mo
  within 14 months; + [[prestige-labs|Prestige Labs]] integration → $4.4M/mo by month 20
  (PDF pp. 193–194).
- Owner distributions: over $43M over five years (PDF p. 21).
- Exit: sold 66% for $46.2M in an all-cash deal (PDF p. 21).
