# How to Get Ahead Faster Right Now Using AI

## Summary

Alex identifies applied AI as the fastest current economic opportunity, but rejects
abstract billion-dollar ideas. He recommends choosing one concrete business function and
using AI to solve a labor- or training-intensive problem. AI simultaneously increases
demand for specialized implementers and supplies leverage through agents and repeated
delivery.

## Key claims

- As of April 2026, Alex names AI as the clearest current path to getting ahead.
- He says AI can be applied to marketing, sales, product, customer success, IT,
  recruiting, HR, finance, and legal work.
- Supply, demand, and leverage make specialized AI skills valuable: business demand is
  high, skilled supply is limited, and tools let one person serve many.
- Alex defines technology as reducing the value of humans in aggregate while increasing
  the value of a human who can use it.
- He recommends learning continuously, selecting one costly business problem, and using
  AI to reduce its headcount or training burden.
- A broad "AI-ify the world" idea has no value until narrowed to a specific function and
  implementation context.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "For right now it's AI, period, full stop, no questions asked."

> "Technology as something that decreases the value of humans, but increases the value of a human."

> "Having the billion-dollar AI idea is worth exactly zero dollars."
