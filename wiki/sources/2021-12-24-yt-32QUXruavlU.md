# Alex Hormozi & Chad Wesley Smith EXCLUSIVE

## Summary

Alex Hormozi hosts Chad Wesley Smith of Juggernaut Training Systems. Chad explains the
origin of Juggernaut, the shift from facility-based training to online content and
coaching, the launch of Juggernaut AI, and the later realization that focus matters more
than trying to run gym, coaching, apparel, supplements, content, and software all at
once. Alex mostly asks questions and draws implications for gym owners. The strongest
Alex-attributed idea is that the coach's role must rise above the workout into broader
health guidance, emotional support, and concierge-like trust.

## Key claims (attributed)

**Alex Hormozi (persona-relevant):**
- Alex introduces Chad as a highly credible strength coach and seminar/training
  influence.
- He asks whether Chad's attention is now singularly on Juggernaut AI, using the company

**Chad Wesley Smith (guest - NOT persona data):**
- He opened his first facility in 2009, wrote *The Juggernaut Method* in 2010, and moved
  more online around 2012.
- Juggernaut's website traffic grew from roughly 25,000 to 250,000 unique visitors per
  month by 2013.
- Juggernaut AI, released in December 2020, is described as a patent-pending expert
  system for powerlifting and powerbuilding that uses questionnaires and performance
  feedback.
- The physical gym was relationship-driven, while online growth came from valuable
  content and being early and credible in powerlifting.
- He later realized that trying to run too many lines at once diluted quality; coaching
  and content were the core.
