## Company profiles

### Gym Launch (Gym Launch Secrets LLC)
- Founded **2016** (self-reported + Wyoming number prefix). Started as in-person
  done-for-you gym turnarounds; pivoted **2017** to a licensing model. Claims: $3M
  profit in first 6 months, $17M in the next 12, "4,500+ facilities in 13 countries."
  All revenue figures **self-reported**.
- **2021**: 66% sold to American Pacific Group (all-cash). Deal **verified** via the
  Capstone Partners announcement (dated 2022-01-18); the **$46.2M** figure appears
  **only** in Hormozi's own posts — APG/Capstone disclosed no financials.
- Still operating as gymlaunch.com; Hormozi no longer featured.

### Prestige Labs
- Supplement line sold through the Gym Launch gym network. Launch year **conflicts**:
  2018 (Capstone deal announcement) vs 2019 (his own posts). Claims $20M first-year
  revenue (**self-reported**). Included in the 2021 APG sale. Site now near-empty.

### ALAN
- AI/SMS lead-nurturing software for local businesses, co-founded ~2019. He states he
  sold **75%** (all-stock) in 2021 to "a strategic partner" (**self-reported**). Site
  usealan.com still live as a marketing page.

### Acquisition.com
- Holding/investment company, formed ~**2020**, scaled after the 2021 sale. He
  describes it as a portfolio of **minority stakes** in founder-owned, cash-flow-
  positive companies. Portfolio revenue claims grew ">$85M/yr" (2021) →
  ">$200–250M/yr" (2024), ~12–16 companies. All **self-reported**. Leila Hormozi is
  CEO; Alex is public-facing managing partner.

### Skool
- Community platform founded 2019 by Sam Ovens & Daniel Kang. Hormozi announced a
  major investment in **Jan 2024** ("biggest investment of my life"); launched the
  Skool Games alongside it. Ownership split (rumored ~50/50 with Ovens) is
  **unverified**.

### Publishing / media
