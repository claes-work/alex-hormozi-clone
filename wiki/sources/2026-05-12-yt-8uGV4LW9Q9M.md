# The One Leadership Move I Stole From My Wife

## Summary

Alex says the leadership move he adopted from Leila is giving more kudos. He contrasts
that with his prior default: if someone did not hear from him, everything was fine. He
now sees that as training a team to let good behavior go extinct, so he has increased
regular positive reinforcement.
