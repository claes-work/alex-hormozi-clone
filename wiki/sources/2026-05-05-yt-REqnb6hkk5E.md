# The Only Referral Strategy Your Community Needs

## Summary

Alex responds to a community owner whose welcome DM unlocks courses when members invite
three niche friends. He approves the tactic and advises not overcomplicating it: find one
referral mechanism that works and hammer it. As an optional layer, he suggests an uncapped
periodic giveaway where every referral gives both the referrer and friend a ticket toward
a grand prize the community values.

## Key claims

- If a referral tactic is already working, do not overcomplicate it; hammer the one that
  works.
- A simple community referral unlock can work when the unlocked courses are valuable.
- A monthly or quarterly uncapped giveaway can sit on top: each referral adds tickets for
  both the referrer and the referred friend.
- The grand prize should match what the community values, such as a day with the creator,
  a business turnaround, a landing page, or coaching sessions.

> "Find one that works really well and I'd just hammer it."
