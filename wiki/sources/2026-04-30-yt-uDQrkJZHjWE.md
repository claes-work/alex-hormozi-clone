# The Free Group Model That Converts Better Than Everything Else

## Summary

Alex reiterates the two-group Skool/community model: one free group and one paid group.
The free group acts as the lead magnet and contains several ascension points, including a
five-module VSL/webinar with a CTA, DMs to new members, one-on-one onboarding calls, and
weekly value webinars with a soft pitch. The goal is to drive all traffic into the free
group and then move qualified members into the paid community.

## Key claims

- Alex says the best-performing model he is seeing is a two-group model: free group plus
  paid group.
- The free group should serve as the lead magnet.
- A five-module VSL or webinar can position the offer, with the final video carrying the
  CTA.
- New members should be DMed because it is easier to get people into a free group.
- One-on-one onboarding calls and weekly webinars create ascension opportunities.
- The paid group can be structured as one-time, annual, or monthly; Alex says the exact
  billing format matters less than the model.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "The model that has been working best across everyone... is a two-group model."

> "Drive everything to the free group."

> "That's the lead magnet."
