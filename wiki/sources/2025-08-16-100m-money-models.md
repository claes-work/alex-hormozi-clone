# $100M Money Models: How To Make Money (2025)

**What it is.** The third book in Hormozi's "$100M Series" (after *$100M Offers*, 2021,
and *$100M Leads*, 2023), published 2025-08-16 by Acquisition.com (copyright Bumble IP,
LLC) with a record-breaking free virtual launch event (see
[[2025-08-16-affiliate-black-book|Affiliate Black Book]]). 214 PDF pages. Raw file:
[raw/2025-08-16-100m-money-models.md](../../raw/2025-08-16-100m-money-models.md)
(verbatim text extracted from the user-purchased PDF; original PDF alongside; cite by
`PDF p. N` markers).

**Reliability note.** All biographical and revenue claims are self-reported by Hormozi;
the one third-party datapoint is Alex Becker (CEO, Hyros) crediting him with "the highest
return on advertising of any business using our advertising tracking platform… by a
mile" among clients spending ≥$250k/yr (PDF pp. 24–25).

## Thesis

Businesses die because acquiring a customer costs more — or pays back slower — than what
the customer returns. The fix is a **Money Model**: "a deliberate sequence of offers"
that makes so much money from each customer inside 30 days that the cost of getting more
customers stops mattering (PDF pp. 23, 194). The trilogy placement: *Offers* = what to
sell, *Leads* = how to find people, *Money Models* = how to get them to buy (PDF p. 204).

## Structure and content

- **Start Here** — his origin story as proof: gym floor "concrete bedroom", the
  storage-unit mentor's "$127 free month" money model, the $25k call ("you have a
  level-10 skill in a level-2 opportunity"), Gym Launch, the $46.2M sale, Acquisition.com
  (PDF pp. 13–23).
- **Section I** — Money Model definition, the rental-car parable ($19/day → $100/day via
  5 sequenced offers), bad-model death spiral, 8x/27x compounding math, the four offer
  types (PDF pp. 24–35).
- **Section II: Attraction Offers** — Win Your Money Back, Giveaways, Decoy Offer, Buy X
  Get Y Free, Pay Less Now or Pay More Later, Free Goodwill (PDF pp. 36–92).
- **Section III: Upsell Offers** — Classic, Menu, Anchor, Rollover (PDF pp. 88–126).
- **Section IV: Downsell Offers** — Payment Plans, Trial With Penalty, Feature Downsells
  (PDF pp. 126–161).
- **Section V: Continuity Offers** — Bonus, Discount, Waived Fee; the 4-week billing
  note he calls the "Highest Value Per Word Note In This Book" (PDF pp. 161–192).
- **Section VI: Make Your Money Model** — three stages, build order, assembly rules
  ("Simple Scales. Fancy Fails."), Gym Launch evolution with revenue milestones, four
  worked example models (PDF pp. 192–202).
- **Ten Years In Ten Minutes** — recap one-liners of all 15 offers (PDF pp. 204–208).
- **Final Thoughts** — motivational close: "You cannot lose if you do not quit", "Be one
  of zero" (PDF pp. 209–211). **Free Goodies** — 9 Acquisition.com resources (PDF
  pp. 212–214).

## Key claims

- A $100M Money Model makes more profit from ONE customer than it costs to get and
  service MANY in the first 30 days — cash stops limiting growth (PDF p. 205).
- Payback speed beats profit ratio; slow payback starves even profitable businesses
  (PDF pp. 29–30).
- 2x value × 2x count × 2x speed = 8x growth; 3x each = 27x (PDF p. 30).
- Build one stage at a time; a "finished" model implemented at once collapses the
  business (PDF p. 195).
- Gym Launch milestones: $476K/mo in 3 months → $1.5M/mo → $2.3M/mo in 14 months →
  $4.4M/mo by month 20 with Prestige Labs (PDF pp. 193–194).
- Keep raising price until you make less money (PDF p. 200).
- "Hard Selling Is For Weak Products"; give instant refunds; obey tightening "free"
  advertising laws (PDF pp. 34–35).
