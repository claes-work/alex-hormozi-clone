# The Real Reason Your Customers Ghost After Buying

## Summary

In this short coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises Veronica White, who sells project-management courses through a Skool community and wants to grow from about $700K to $5M. Her traffic is mostly TikTok Live, and churn is 12-14% on a $99 offer. Alex diagnoses the missing piece as an ascension and activation motion: every buyer should get structured onboarding, a follow-up call, and a higher-value offer tied to the next problem after learning project management: getting a job.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - the coach. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible.
- **Veronica White** - the coachee. Her revenue, offer, traffic source, price point, conversion, and churn are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- A $99 community with 12-14% churn and roughly 5% page conversion does not first need more TikTok Live volume; it needs activation and ascension.
- All buyers should receive a free call with someone on the team after onboarding, then a second touch about seven days later.
- The call sequence should reduce churn, increase activation, and create an upsell opportunity.
- The more expensive offer should not depend on the founder's one-on-one time if the goal is to reach $5M.
- The natural next problem after project-management training is job placement; helping graduates get jobs is valuable enough to support an upsell.
- A job-placement layer can be built by scraping project-manager job boards, contacting companies, and matching companies with trained community members.
