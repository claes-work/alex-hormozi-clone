# I Bought 32 Teeth Whitening Studios. Here's Why

## Summary

Alex explains how he looks for 10x opportunities by mapping a business from click to
close to renewal and identifying breakages against known benchmarks. He says he recently
bought a 32-location teeth-whitening studio chain because the sales process revealed
simple levers: convert one-time buyers to membership, adjust packages and prices, add an
intake questionnaire, and increase LTV. He also self-reports selling his house, cars,
and three companies in 2021 because money was cheap and valuations seemed high.

## Key claims (Alex's self-report and advice)

- Alex evaluates a business like a pipeline from attention to leads, appointments,
  shows, closes, cash collected, delivery, renewal, and upsell.
- He looks for breakages where his team has tested benchmarks and processes.
- Multiplicative improvements across show rate, pricing, churn, and LTV can reveal a
  10x opportunity.
- Confidence depends on how easy the changes are to implement.
- Alex self-reports buying a 32-location teeth-whitening studio chain.
- He says changing the sales process, membership model, packages, pricing, and intake
  could 5x customer LTV.
- Doubling locations over five years, combined with LTV gains, could create a 10x.
- Alex says whether to sell or hold depends on the business, next growth increment,
  owner fit, horizon, and offers.
- He self-reports selling his house, cars, and three companies in 2021 because money was
  cheap and buyers were paying high prices.
