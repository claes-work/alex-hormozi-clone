# How to Build a Powerful Network From Scratch

## Summary

Alex says his peer network has grown more organically than deliberately, but his useful
position sits at the intersection of content, creators, investing, money, and business.
He argues that understanding both the creator world and the investing/business world
creates advantages on both sides: creators need monetization, and investors/business
owners want deal flow. His practical networking posture is to help first, collect
future IOUs, and "dig the well before" he is thirsty.

## Key claims (Alex's self-report and advice)

- Alex says his peer group has not been built through a highly deliberate mastermind
  strategy.
- He positions himself at the intersection of content and business/investing.
- Understanding creator monetization and investor deal flow creates advantage on both
  sides.
- Alex says he tries to help people when he can because future IOUs can become valuable
  in times of need.
- His stated networking strategy is to give first and build relationship capital before
  needing it.
- Alex says he is trying to get deeper into technology and AI because he sees ignorance
  debt there.
