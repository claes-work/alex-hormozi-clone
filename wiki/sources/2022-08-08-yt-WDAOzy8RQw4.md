## Key claims
- Gym Launch's frameworks have been used to scale gyms to over $100k/month, refined over six years working with 4,500+ gyms across the world.
- The free download is organized into six sections (the "7 money models" living inside them):
  1. **The three levels of gyms** — a segmentation of where a gym sits.
  2. **Solving the acquisition problem** — systems/frameworks for getting new clients.
  3. **Solving the churn problem** — retention systems; churn is cited as a top reason most gyms cannot scale past $50k/month and get stuck in "no man's land" as a "leaky bucket."
  4. **Expansion revenue** — making each client worth more via ascension to higher service tiers and selling products/supplements; positioned as critical for gyms in the $20k–$50k/month range aiming for $1M/year.
  5. **Building your dream team** — finding, identifying, interviewing, hiring, onboarding, training, retaining, and communicating with staff.
  6. **Macro business models** — the higher-level models every business needs; claimed to have helped Gym Launch scale to over $100M in revenue over six years.
- A gym with neither an acquisition system nor a churn (retention) system stays stuck because it turns people out "like crazy" and cannot outpace the losses.
- The PDF is a free lead magnet accessed via the link in the video description; the video series does a full breakdown of each section.
