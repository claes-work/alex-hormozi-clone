# "How Can I Use AI to Get More Customers?" | Hormozi Hotline

## Summary
Short Hormozi Hotline clip. A caller in Alex's Acquisition group asks how he would use "Acquisition AI" (ACQ AI, Alex's in-house tool) to increase the *volume* of his Core Four outreach activities, noting his messaging is primarily one-to-one or one-to-many via DM, text, or email. Alex reframes the question away from raw volume: for this caller the biggest "bang for your buck" is not automating the sending but improving the **messaging and the message flow itself** — the offer structure, the sales process, and the conversion sequence. His guiding question is whether there are things you can *inject into the process* that convert a higher percentage of the people you're already messaging and talking to. Alex is explicit about the tool's current limits: doing the actual multi-step outbound work is "beyond the scope of ACQ AI as it currently sits today" — that would require multi-step third-party workflows he wasn't going to cover. When the caller restates the takeaway — that AI helps most by producing VSLs or other trust-building assets to place *between* the calls and texts — Alex agrees emphatically ("100%"). The practical thesis: use AI to sharpen conversion (better messages, offers, and mid-funnel trust assets), not to blast more volume. Ends with the standard closing promo for his free 10-stage zero-to-$100M+ roadmap.
