# The Irresistible Referral Offer That Makes Partners Sell for You Without Asking

## Summary

Alex coaches a payroll-services owner with 300 clients and roughly 30 CPA referral
partners. He says the business likely does not need to niche down yet; it should
advertise more to the existing referral-node type, make an irresistible offer to CPAs,
and integrate into their customer journey so referrals happen by process rather than
memory.

## Key claims

- Alex says niching should solve a specific problem, not be done for its own sake.
- He says niching is an efficiency solution: it can raise lifetime gross profit, reduce
  CAC, and make advertising more specific.
- He says this payroll business already has two valuable traits: customers who do not
  stop buying and partners who can keep selling.
- He recommends going directly to CPAs rather than direct-to-business advertising.
- He says most referral programs fail because a generic 20% referral fee is not
  irresistible enough.
- He recommends integrating the service into the CPA's customer journey so the CPA's
  customers are exposed to the payroll service by default.
- He gives an example of a CPA offering free LLC/bank-account setup because bookkeeping
  upsells make the front-end service worthwhile.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "You don't want to niche for the sake of niching."

> "What's the irresistible offer to the affiliate?"

> "You can sell my service at 100% margin."
