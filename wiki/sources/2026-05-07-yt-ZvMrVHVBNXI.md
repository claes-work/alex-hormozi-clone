# The 3 Things That Make a Man Attractive

## Summary

Chris Williamson introduces Robert Glover's frame that an attractive man is
comfortable in his own skin, knows where he is going, and has fun while going there.
Alex endorses the frame and expands it: direction, internal comfort, and fun are each
attractive on their own, though he personally prefers the first two over the third.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2026-05-07)

- Alex strongly agrees with the three-part attractiveness frame introduced by Chris.
- He says direction in life, internal comfort, and fun are strong ideals.
- He says a person who knows where he is going can attract others even without much fun
  or comfort.
- He says visible self-comfort alone can also be attractive.
- He says fun alone can still attract people, though he would prefer direction and
  comfort.
