# How to Get More Patients for Your Dental Clinic

## Summary

In this short answer, Alex Hormozi advises a dental clinic targeting abroad patients with lower prices than their home countries. He recommends a content-first strategy built around before-and-after proof, similar to Turkish hair-transplant clinics. The operational key is to build content capture into the customer journey: take photos at intake and follow-up points, turn each patient into potential proof, explain the case with pain-based hooks, then use the best-performing organic content as ads with CTAs.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Dental tourism can advertise through outbound, content, or ads, but Alex's bet is content first.
- Before-and-after proof is the natural content engine for visible transformation services.
- Content capture must be built into the customer journey, not treated as a separate afterthought.
- Every patient can become a potential before-and-after proof asset.
- The clinic can explain cases anonymously or with identifying details covered if needed.
- Alex does not love winning on price, but lower price can be part of the strategy in some markets.
- The best path is to find winning content first, add a CTA, then run paid ads behind the winner.
