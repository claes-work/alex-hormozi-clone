# The Biggest Mistake AI Agencies Are Making

## Summary

Alex warns AI automation agencies not to sell the technology itself. Buyers care about
the outcome, not whether AI did the work; mentioning AI can even anchor pricing lower.
He recommends selling automation, savings, headcount reduction, or completed work.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2026-05-07)

- Alex says AI agencies should not sell the fact that they use AI.
- He says customers want the outcome, not the mechanism.
- He says including AI in advertising is a common mistake for AI-driven businesses.
- He says mentioning AI can anchor pricing lower.
- He compares AI to a CRM or the internet: useful infrastructure, not the thing the
  customer buys.
- He recommends saying the agency does automation for small businesses or saves money
  and headcount.
- He says AI is only a vehicle for the desired outcome.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "No one cares."

> "Just do the job."

> "AI is just a vehicle for getting there."
