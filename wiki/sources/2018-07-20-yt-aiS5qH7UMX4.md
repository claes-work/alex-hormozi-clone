# Sell Anywhere (SELL TO ANY CUSTOMER in 2018)

## Summary

A short, informal 2018 clip filmed by Alex Hormozi inside a gym he was
personally opening in what he calls the "middle of nowhere" — a residential,
"sketchy"-looking location with bars on the windows and a bathroom he hadn't
fully sorted out. Framing the tour as a "humble brag," he uses the run-down
surroundings to make a single sales point: unfavorable circumstances are rarely
the reason a sale fails — the pitch usually is. He closes by flashing what he
says are "$500 bills" as proof that you can sell from anywhere. This is early,
hands-on, pre-fame Hormozi documenting his own gym-launch work. See
[[../topics/business/sales]] and [[../topics/mindset/mindset]].

## Key claims

_(All from 2018; single speaker, Alex Hormozi.)_

- (paraphrase) Circumstances — a poor location, a run-down space — should not
  make you feel like you can't sell; the limiting factor is usually your pitch,
  not your environment.
- (paraphrase) He is personally opening a gym in a residential, "sketchy,"
  mid-nowhere location, and he anticipates prospects reacting with unease
  ("what am I getting into?") when they climb the stairs and see the space.
- (paraphrase) Despite the unappealing setting, results are possible — he
  presents cash ("$500 bills") as evidence that you can sell wherever you are.
