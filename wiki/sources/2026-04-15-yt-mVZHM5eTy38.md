# Stop Worrying About AI and Start Using It

## Summary

Alex tells a professional-advice business owner that AI is not an immediate existential
threat to the space. His frame is humans plus technology: humans plus internet, humans plus
computers, humans plus AI. Until AI beats humans at everything, the behavior change is to
use it as a tool; if it beats humans at everything, the distinction stops mattering.

## Key claims

- On 2026-04-15, Alex says AI in professional advice is humans plus technology, not a
  separate category from earlier technology waves.
- He says people who use AI better will gain an advantage because they use superior tools.
- He says expert judgment still matters because someone needs enough context to evaluate
  the output.
- He says AI writing is better than most people but not better than the best writers as of
  this clip.
- He says his anxiety frame is to keep embracing technology until AI beats humans at
  everything, in which case everyone is in the same situation.
- He says the practical behavior change is not worrying about disruption but harnessing AI.
