# The 4 RAREST skills on earth that make BILLIONAIRES...

## Summary

Alex names four scarce, high-value skills that compound at scale: raising and multiplying capital, capturing and multiplying attention, returning time to other people at no incremental cost, and influencing behavior at mass scale. He maps them to leverage through capital, media, technology, and labor/actions.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2021-12-13)

- Alex says the scarcest skills should be judged by degree of mastery, not as binary yes/no skills.
- He says raising and multiplying capital can create unlimited fortunes when done at scale.
- He says capturing and multiplying attention is the media skill behind large platforms and audiences.
- He says returning time to others at no incremental cost is the technological/software path to wealth.
- He says influencing behavior at mass scale changes downstream actions and can create money or power.
- He maps the four skills to the leverage types of capital, media, technology/code, and other people's actions.
- He says each skill compounds rather than merely adding linearly.
