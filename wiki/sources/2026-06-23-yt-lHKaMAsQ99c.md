# Why Cost-Plus Pricing Is Killing Your Roofing Business

## Summary

In this short coaching answer, Alex Hormozi advises a small family roofing business. He says the constraint is cash flow, money models, and sales, then attacks cost-plus pricing common in home services. Alex argues that customers do not care what the job costs the contractor; instead, roofing businesses should price around what customers fear most and guarantee against downside such as over-budget projects, slow work, and long-term failure.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- The business must solve cash flow first.
- For roofing and home services, the problem is money models and sales.
- Home-service businesses overuse cost-plus pricing.
- Customers do not care what the job costs the contractor.
- Cost-plus pricing can create a disincentive to reduce costs because absolute profit rises with higher costs.
- Customers are often most afraid that the job will go over budget, break later, or take too long.
- The business should guarantee against one or more of those downside vectors so the customer sees a different, lower-risk offer.
- Customers will pay a premium for insurance against the fear that matters to them.
