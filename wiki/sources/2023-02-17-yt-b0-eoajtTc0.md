## Summary
A Gym Launch client-story episode filmed at Grit Life Fitness, where a Gym Launch coach interviews owner Lindsay Mattingly about her path from sleeping at a gym she managed to running a ~$39k/month facility, then coaches her live on conversion, pricing, churn, and paying herself. ## Key claims
- Lindsay slept at a gym she managed while relocating (no housing) and later opened Grit Life Fitness in 2020 with ~$40k all-in (~$10k savings + $30k loan); ~86–106 members, ~$39k/month, ~20% margin at the time of filming.
