# The Most Slept-On Marketing Strategy

## Summary

Alex advises a residential security-assessment provider to market through
demonstration. Instead of only claiming expertise, the provider should walk through
neighborhoods, show security mistakes, record the work, post it on LinkedIn, and start
free if necessary to earn experience, proof, testimonials, and referrals.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2026-05-07)

- Alex says demonstration is underused in marketing.
- He recommends showing the actual work instead of merely claiming competence.
- He suggests walking around neighborhoods and pointing out visible security mistakes
  as content.
- He says the content can be posted on LinkedIn for B2B marketing.
- He says live demonstration creates proof because the audience can see the expertise
  happen.
- He recommends doing free work at the start if that is what gets experience,
  testimonials, referrals, and potential customers.
- He says a beginner's time is effectively worth zero if they have no customers, so
  getting non-cash assets from free work can still be rational.
- He says free work also softens the blow if the provider is not yet good.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Demonstrate you doing what you do."

> "Do it. And record it."

> "Start for free."
