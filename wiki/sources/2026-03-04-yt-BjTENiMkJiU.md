# Why I Like to Work Out

## Summary
Short interview clip. Asked (by an off-camera interviewer) whether he intentionally leaves anything in his life "messy" to stay human, Alex answers: training. He's worked out for 20-plus years and stayed consistent through seasons of loving it and seasons of "just doing it" to keep what he has. The realization of this year (2026) was giving himself "carte blanche" to work out **whenever he wants, for as long as he wants** — he prefers long, multi-hour morning workouts, done with someone he likes, ideally followed by eating together afterward. That combination, he says, is "the beginning of an amazing day" and makes the whole day "a W." He frames this as a use of wealth: he now has enough money that training the way he wants is a permitted luxury, and he spends "an egregious amount" on his home gym — more than he spent on all six commercial gyms he owned put together — stocking his favorite pieces from manufacturers around the world. The gift of the year was giving himself permission to train that way **guilt-free**. Ends with the acquisition.com/roadmap scaling-roadmap promo.
