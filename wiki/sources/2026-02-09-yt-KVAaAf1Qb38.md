# How to Make Content That Promotes Your Business

## Summary
In a short @MoreMozi Q&A, a viewer building a finance-department automation business (targeting companies of 20–200 people) asks what LinkedIn content to make. Alex Hormozi's answer is a concrete content-strategy playbook: talk about what you know, one topic at a time. Enumerate every micro-task a finance department performs (he estimates ~150 repeated tasks), turn that list into a content schedule, and produce one piece of content per task explaining how to automate it. The people who read and watch that specialized content are precisely the buyers for the service. He argues against hoarding "secrets" — finance buyers don't want to learn the tech stack, they just want proof it works — and recommends short 5–10-second demonstration clips of a process running/automating in the background to show the result.
