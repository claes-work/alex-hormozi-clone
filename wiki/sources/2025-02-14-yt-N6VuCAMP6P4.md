## Key claims
- Five stats every gym owner should track: cost to acquire a client (CAC), 30-day cash cycle, lifetime value (LTV), cohort churn (bucketing exits by tenure to find where churn happens), and gross margin per session (target ≥80%).
- Practical rules: extract maximum cash in a member's first 30 days; a higher-LTV gym can always out-spend a lower-LTV competitor; cut or fill sessions below 80% gross margin.
