# Helping a Serivce Business Double Revenue

## Summary

Alex coaches a French fertility-education business doing $1M that wants $3M. The business
currently runs organic launches for two products. Rather than doubling visible organic
launch cadence and stressing the front-woman brand, Alex validates using paid ads to the
warm audience to run "stealth" launches behind the front message.

## Key claims

- On 2026-04-16, Alex says doubling the organic launch cadence could be hard when the
  business already has a public cadence and a front-woman brand.
- He validates running paid ads to the existing warm audience behind the scenes.
- He agrees that the founder can use paid warm-audience campaigns to increase launch
  frequency without visibly doubling the front-facing organic push.
- The clip is a narrow tactical coaching answer; most details are founder-supplied.

> "If you already have a cadence on organic, doubling that might be tough."
