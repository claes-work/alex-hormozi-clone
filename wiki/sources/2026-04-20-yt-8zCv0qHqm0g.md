# How I Structure My Day to Get More Work Done

## Summary

Alex says he is not a full-time content creator, despite having a large content team.
He describes mornings as maker time for portfolio decisions, models, research, concepts,
and book/content outlines; afternoons are meeting time. He books meetings back-to-front
so the largest uninterrupted block stays earlier in the day.
