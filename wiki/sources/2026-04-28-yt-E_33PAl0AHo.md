# Your $90 CPA Is Killing You (Here's How to Fix It)

## Summary

Alex advises a $22/month community with a $90 CPA to fix economics through ascension, not
just ad angles. At that price, the operator needs either much higher conversion or a way
to move a percentage of members into a higher-priced offer. He repeats the four community
conversion points: onboarding call, DMs, weekly Q&A/soft-pitch call, and a scripted
VSL/webinar module sequence.

## Key claims

- A $22/month membership with a $90 CPA is weak unless LTV is much higher than the likely
  churn-adjusted value.
- Alex says the operator must ascend some customers into a more expensive offer.
- Running ads to a free group may lower CPA if the free group ascends into a paid group.
- Four conversion points are onboarding calls, DMs, weekly Q&A/webinars with soft CTA,
  and a VSL/webinar sequence in the modules.
- The VSL sequence should include origin/opportunity, concerns about conditions, concerns
  about other people, concerns about the buyer's own capability, and a CTA.
- Alex says the paid group can likely be priced higher, such as around $99, in this model.
