# Helping a Janitorial Business Get More Leads

## Summary

In this short coaching answer, Alex Hormozi advises a janitorial business asking what lead magnet to use. Alex says the lead magnet matters less than the acquisition channel and offer. For local janitorial services, he recommends Google PPC for high-intent local searches, door knocking for smaller buildings, LinkedIn outbound to building managers for larger buildings, and cash-forward terms such as quarterly payment in advance.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- The target customer depends on building size: smaller buildings may mean business owners, while larger buildings mean building managers.
- Google PPC is likely the fastest first source of leads for local janitorial intent because searchers are qualified, even if expensive.
- Door knocking can work for smaller buildings.
- LinkedIn outbound to building managers can work better for larger buildings and whale accounts.
- In this kind of business, the lead magnet matters less than the offer.
- Alex would focus on a compelling way to pull cash forward, such as quarterly payment in advance.
- Janitorial churn may be low, so the main game is customer acquisition.
