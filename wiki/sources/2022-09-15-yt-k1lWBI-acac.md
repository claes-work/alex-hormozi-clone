## Key claims (attributed)

All claims below are **Kale Owen's / Gym Launch's** (model credited to Alex Hormozi):
- The "broken gym model" (95% of gyms) has one service layer and no front-end cash ("icing"), leaving it fragile and capped at ~$12–18k/month with no profit.

- Selling two of ten clients into $600 semi-private doubles monthly value-per-customer from $100 to $200.
- The wedding-cake layers stack to $100k+/month: large group (~$40k), hybrid nutrition+accountability (~$25k more), semi-private ($500–1000/mo EFT), internal plays every 4–6 weeks, and Prestige Labs supplements (~$80/client/month commission).
