# Why Being Weird Is Your Real Superpower

## Summary

Alex argues that the least cringe path is being oneself, even when that means being
weird and losing some social or romantic opportunities. He self-reports that many dates
did not fit his peculiarities, but Leila did because she could see and discuss the
business mechanics he naturally noticed.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2026-05-07)

- Alex says the most un-cringe thing is being oneself.
- He self-reports that some women may have been turned away by how weird he was.
- He says he wanted someone who accepted and was aligned with his peculiarities.
- He says exceptional people will be different and will be seen as weird.
- He says an exceptional person may not get second dates, but may not want those second
  dates either.
- He self-reports a first date with Leila at a frozen-yogurt store where they both
  noticed the business model.
- He says pretending to be someone else can attract someone who falls in love with a
  role rather than the real person.
- He says pretending every hour of every day is profoundly lonely.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "The most un-cringe thing is you being you."

> "You see this, too?"

> "There's nothing that can be more lonely than pretending."
