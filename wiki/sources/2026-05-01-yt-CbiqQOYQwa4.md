# Keep Raising Your Price Until You're Closing 1 Out of 3

## Summary

Alex reviews an offer where a podcast interview converts ideal clients into a VSL offer
with a JV upside model. He says the founder can move some compensation from future upside
to cash up front by taking a fee first, letting the client recoup first proceeds, and
then sharing upside after that. If the offer is truly converting at or near 100%, Alex
recommends raising price until the close rate falls to about one in three.

## Key claims

- If a JV or upside offer converts extremely well, the founder can add cash up front and
  give the client preference on early proceeds.
- A structure could collect an upfront fee, return the first dollars generated to the
  client, then split upside after that.
- Alex likes the podcast-to-VSL offer if the founder can execute it.
- When an offer closes at 100%, Alex recommends raising the price until the close rate is
  around one out of three.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Just drag some of that back cash to the front."

> "Keep raising the price until you're closing one out of three."
