# The Single Greatest Opportunity for 2026

## Summary

In a short Q&A, Alex Hormozi distinguishes macro opportunities from smaller land grabs. In his lifetime, he names the internet, social/Web 2.0, and AI as the three macro opportunities. Under those sit micro opportunities like Facebook ads, Google search, and TikTok Shop. He says opportunities only pay if the operator has the skills to exploit them, and he still sees social media/brand-building as unusually valuable because people can advertise themselves and get paid to do it.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Macro opportunities in Alex's lifetime include the internet, social/Web 2.0, and AI.
- Micro opportunities appear inside those waves, such as Facebook ads, Google search, or TikTok Shop.
- TikTok Shop in 2026 resembles an early Amazon-style land grab because of supply-demand imbalance between creators who want monetization and products needing distribution.
- Market land grabs create outsized returns until the market stabilizes.
- Capitalizing on opportunities requires skill; opportunity alone is insufficient.
- Social media remains a major opportunity for people who are good because it lets them build a brand while being paid to distribute attention.
- Alex avoids public valuation claims for illiquid private assets because enterprise value depends on the buyer and timing.
