# My Actual Daily Schedule

## Summary

Alex describes his usual day as "Alex time" from roughly 5 a.m. to 1 p.m., meetings from
about 1 p.m. to 4 or 4:30 p.m., then gym, food, and ending the day. He says Las Vegas is
the hub, though he and the team travel often. He also explains that his early janky
content style was mostly how it started: he clicked record, put files in Dropbox, and had
the team upload them. Better audio/video later improved objective performance, and he
sees adapting format to the audience's attention context as part of making value
available.

## Key claims

- Alex says most days he works on his own material from about 5 a.m. to 1 p.m.
- Meetings usually happen from about 1 p.m. to 4 or 4:30 p.m.
- After meetings he goes to the gym, eats, and is done.
- Las Vegas is the hub, but he and the team travel frequently.
- His early content was intentionally simple in practice: record, send to Dropbox, and
  have the team upload.
- He believes in starting before the path is clear.
- Improved production quality later helped videos perform objectively better.
- Alex says he adapts value delivery to the context people consume in instead of
  complaining about attention spans.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Usually 5:00 to 1:00 is Alex time."

> "Just start, and the path illuminates as you walk."

> "There's a hell of a lot you can cover in 60 seconds if you know what you're saying."
