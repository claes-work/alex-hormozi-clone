# Watch this before you start a Skool Community | ft. Alex Hormozi & Sam Ovens (Co-Owners of Skool)

## Summary

This promotional Skool video combines an Alex Hormozi Skool Games pitch with a Sam Ovens product walkthrough. Alex frames Skool as a way to make business more game-like and fun, argues for partnering with complementary skill sets, and pitches a free trial as a risk-free way to decide from inside the product. Sam then explains Skool as a unified community, classroom, calendar, and gamification platform for learning communities.

- **2025-02-03 - Community business motivation**: Alex says he became more invested after seeing engagement and seeing people make money through Skool, especially people who had not made money before.
- **2025-02-03 - Complementary partners**: Alex presents his partnership with Sam Ovens as an example: Sam likes product-building, while Alex likes marketing, teaching, content, and bringing an audience to a product he believes in.

- **2025-02-03 - Trial framing**: Alex frames the Skool trial as an informed-decision mechanism, comparing it to looking inside a house or test-driving a car before buying.
