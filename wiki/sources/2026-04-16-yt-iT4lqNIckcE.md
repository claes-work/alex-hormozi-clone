# How to 4X Your Sales Without Spending More on Leads

## Summary

Alex gives a forceful instruction to call new leads within 60 seconds. He treats lead
response time as a diagnostic of business excellence underneath LTV:CAC. Slow response
lets intent cool, gives prospects time to shop competitors, lowers close rates, raises CAC,
and compresses gross margin.

## Key claims

- On 2026-04-16, Alex says businesses should call leads within 60 seconds.
- He says slow lead response can make the business pay up to four times more per customer
  than necessary.
- He says waiting lets the buyer cool off, compare competitors, and push the business into
  a price war.
- He says LTV:CAC is the "soul" number for a business, and lead response time sits
  underneath it as a key operational driver.
- He says if faster response can 4x sales or divide CAC by four, the business can afford
  the person or system required to do it.
- He frames lead speed as a high-return, minimal-effort rule of thumb.
