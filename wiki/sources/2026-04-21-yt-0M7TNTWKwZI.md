# Is There Anything You're Unhappy About?

## Summary

Alex says his main entrepreneurial complaint is wanting things to happen faster, but he
tries to adjust expectations by asking whether he is manufacturing dissatisfaction. He
says he likes his life, has built an environment around people and work he enjoys, and
avoids sleep/supplement superstitions that become performance crutches.

## Key claims

- Alex says he has the common entrepreneur complaint of wanting things to happen faster.
- He says he checks whether he is simply readjusting expectations to stay dissatisfied.
- He says, self-reported on 2026-04-21, that he likes his life and has worked to create
  an environment with people and activities he enjoys.
- He says he wakes without an alarm and sleeps as long as he can, but sometimes wakes
  early because he is mentally excited.
- He says sleep trackers made him more competitive and superstitious, so they harmed
  sleep more than they helped.
- He warns against crutches that make functioning worse when removed.
