# How I Responded to a VC Who Bashed My Clip

## Summary

reputation filter. He says he uses peace of mind as an indicator that he may be
breaching his values. He tells a story about a venture capitalist who posted a critical
clip of him; Alex privately challenged the criticism and says the person admitted he had
overstepped in pursuit of a viral clip.
