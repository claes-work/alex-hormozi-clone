# The #1 Thing That Changed My Life Wasn't a Course

## Summary

Alex Hormozi says courses, books, workshops, and seminars changed his life, and he calls
himself a product of alternative education. But he says the biggest step-function
changes came from networks and rooms with people ahead of him, where his beliefs about
what was possible broke. He distinguishes procedural knowledge from courses and
declarative possibility-expanding knowledge from association with more advanced people.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Alex says he has no reservations about being a product of the alternative education
  world.
- Courses and DIY materials are useful for procedural knowledge: how to do specific
  tasks.
- Networks and rooms with more advanced people changed Alex's life most.
- Association with people ahead of him broke beliefs about what was possible.
- Incremental gains came from tactical skills; step-function gains came from people
  showing him a bigger future path.
- Alex would like to shadow people such as Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and Jeff Bezos to
  understand their decision frameworks.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "I am a product of the alternative education world."

> "The biggest things that have really changed my life... have been networks of people."

> "That's where you learn about stuff that you didn't know was possible."
