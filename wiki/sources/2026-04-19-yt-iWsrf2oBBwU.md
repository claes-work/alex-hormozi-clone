# How to Make Content Nobody Can Copy

## Summary

Alex says the best way to beat copycats is to get bigger and make them irrelevant by
comparison. In content, he says copycats need him to iterate while he does not need them,
because his source material comes from his actual life and meetings. He advises creators
to find their own original source of content so they are not dependent on someone else.

## Key claims

- Alex says the way to beat people who copy is to get bigger rather than fight them
  directly.
- He says copycats require the original creator to iterate, while the original creator
  does not require the copycats.
- He says copycats can at best become the second-best version of the person they copy.
- He says his content is original because it is documented from real life, such as CEO
  meetings and live business events.
- He says creators need to find their own original source of content.
- He says long-term content games cannot depend on someone else if the point is to keep
  the game going.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "They need me, I don't need them."

> "Find what your original source is."

> "You'll never be first."
