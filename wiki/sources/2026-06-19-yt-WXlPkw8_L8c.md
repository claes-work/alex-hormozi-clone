# Helping an Online Coaching Business Scale Past $1M

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises the same online piano-course seller covered in [[2026-06-20-yt-gaNkOL513jo]]. He repeats the core recommendation: conversion optimization is a low-risk lift, but the likeliest route from $1.4 million toward $3.6 million is paid advertising to the much larger warm audience created by the owner's existing YouTube content. Alex again frames modern platforms as interest media where subscribers are only a small subset of actual content consumers.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - the coach. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible, but duplicates [[2026-06-20-yt-gaNkOL513jo]].
- **Coachee** - online piano-course seller. His price point, automated YouTube funnel, subscriber base, options, and revenue goal are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Conversion-rate optimization may double a funnel but will not create a 10x.
- Paid ads to the existing warm audience are likely the lowest-risk growth path for this goal.
- Follower and subscriber counts matter less than total interest signals under modern algorithms.
- Ads can reach both subscribers and non-subscribing lurkers who have already consumed the creator's content.
- Warm-audience funnel metrics should outperform true cold traffic because the audience already knows and trusts the creator.
- Alex recommends learning paid ads through multiple channels at once: courses, free material, an agency, and agency knowledge transfer.
