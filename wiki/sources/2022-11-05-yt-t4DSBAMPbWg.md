# If We Had To Start A Gym FROM SCRATCH, This Is How We Would Do it

## Summary
Kale Owen and Ed Turney (both former gym owners, now Gym Launch) each name three things they would do differently if starting a gym today — higher/premium pricing, semi-private-focused models, going online sooner, VIP supplement events, lean two-coach teams, and 28-day billing. It is a two-host Gym Launch discussion, not Alex Hormozi content.

## Speaker & attribution
Speakers are Kale Owen ("Kale One") and Ed Turney. Alex Hormozi is referenced in the third person (e.g., "Alex brought it up in 2019" re: the hybrid model). This is **not** Alex Hormozi speaking. `attribution: not-alex`.

## Key claims
- Both would price far higher from the start (e.g., $99–$199/week), lead with semi-private, use large group only as a funnel, and move online sooner to expand their radius.
- VIP/supplement events cited: a client reportedly sold $25,000 in supplements in one day to five people at a VIP event; ~125 clients at right pricing = ~$1M/year.
- Recommend lean teams (two committed coaches), 28-day billing, and capping members at 3 visits/week.
