# Are You Happy?

## Summary

Alex says he is "stoked" and defines happiness as doing what one likes with people one
likes as much as possible. For him, the core ingredient is autonomy: the freedom to do
what he finds interesting, even as the specific work changes.

## Key claims

- Alex says, self-reported on 2026-04-21, that he is very happy.
- He says autonomy has always been the central ingredient: doing what he finds
  interesting.
- He says the specific work can change while the freedom to choose remains the center.
- He says his freedom has not fundamentally changed recently, so his contentedness is
  similar to the prior year.
- He defines happiness simply as doing what one likes with people one likes, as much as
  possible.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "I'm stoked."

> "For me, it's always been about autonomy."

> "Doing what you like to do with people you like."
