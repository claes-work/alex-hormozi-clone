## Key claims (attributed)

All claims below are **Kale Owen's / Gym Launch's**, not Alex's:
- Four pillars of lead nurture: **speed, volume, personalization, availability.**
- Contact leads within 5 minutes of opt-in (ideally <90 seconds); call first, double-dial, then text — never leave a voicemail.
- Book appointments within a 24–48h window (72h at worst); show rate decays as the appointment moves further from opt-in.
- Volume cadence: days 1–5 reach out 3x/day (AM, afternoon, evening); days 6–10 twice/day; days 11–20 once/day; days 21–30 end-of-day.
- Personalize across multiple channels (text, call, email, Facebook/Instagram DM); a "first name + question mark" text is a reliable re-engagement opener.
- Availability: offer sales appointments every day, M–F 9a–12p and 3–7p, with 30-minute booking slots.
