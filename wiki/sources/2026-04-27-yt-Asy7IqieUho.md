# How to Increase Enterprise Value in Your Business

## Summary

Alex says converting a service business into SaaS does not automatically increase
enterprise value. Software is valuable only because good software businesses often have
high gross margins, high revenue retention, and strong growth or viral dynamics. A
software business without those characteristics may be less valuable than the service
business because it adds development complexity without the economics that investors pay
for.

## Key claims

- Software is not inherently valuable, and service businesses are not inherently
  unvaluable.
- Valuable SaaS businesses usually have high gross margins, high revenue retention, and
  strong growth or a viral coefficient.
- Any business with those characteristics can be attractive to investors.
- A SaaS business with poor retention, poor margins, and no growth is not automatically
  valuable.
- Adding software can make a business more operationally complex because the company now
  must maintain a development team.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Software is not inherently valuable."

> "The thing that makes software valuable are business characteristics."

> "Software does not inherently make anything valuable."
