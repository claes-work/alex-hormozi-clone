# How to Recruit A-Players for Your Roofing Company

## Summary

In this short Q&A, Alex Hormozi answers a new roofing-company owner who is doing the Rule of 100 and wants to bring someone else on to sell. Alex says the founder is thinking like a level-one salesperson and needs to progress from selling personally to recruiting a salesperson and then recruiting a sales team. The identity shift is from "sales god" to sales trainer and sales leader.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - solo coaching answer. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible.
- **Questioner** - new roofing-company owner. His start date and Rule-of-100 activity are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- The first level is selling personally.
- The second level is recruiting someone else who can sell.
- The third level is recruiting and leading a team of people who can sell.
- Founders who think they are great salespeople are still only level-one salespeople until they can train others.
- The founder should transition identity from salesperson to sales trainer and sales leader.
- Recruiting a door-to-door salesperson can be as simple as offering commission and teaching the door-knocking method.
