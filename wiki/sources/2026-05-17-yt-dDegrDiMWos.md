# How to Be 100% Authentic in Your Business and Life

## Summary

Alex defines authenticity as behavior when there is no chance of punishment. He says
private behavior and private thoughts reveal the authentic self, and action is
authentic to the extent that words and behavior match those thoughts. Because private
desires or behaviors may violate cultural norms, he treats authenticity as a continuum
rather than a binary.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Alex defines authenticity as how someone behaves when there is no chance of
  punishment.
- Private behavior and private thoughts are closer to the authentic self because no one
  else can punish them.
- Words and actions are authentic when they match private thoughts.
- If private thoughts or behaviors violate norms, full public authenticity may not be
  possible or wise.
- Authenticity should be treated as a continuum rather than a yes/no label.
- Most people should strive to be more authentic rather than claim total authenticity.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Authenticity... is how you behave when you have no chance of punishment."

> "There are degrees of authenticity."

> "Seeing it as a continuum rather than a binary."
