# Helping a $260K Recruiting Business Get to $1M

## Summary

Alex recommends adding a placement success fee to a $6,000 job-search service and
raising it through market feedback. Rather than switch from proven LinkedIn outbound to
paid ads, he advises scaling outbound through more volume or adjacent platforms because
changing platforms is easier than changing acquisition methods.

## Key claims

- As of April 2026, Alex calls price the strongest profit lever and recommends testing a
  success fee, increasing it until buyers reject it.
- He suggests roughly one month of the placed candidate's pay as a starting success fee.
- Alex expects sub-$1,000 CAC for this job opportunity and sees the reported roughly
  $1,800-$2,000 CAC as high; all business metrics are owner-reported and unverified.
- He recommends continuing outbound rather than adding ads when 70%-80% of acquisition
  already comes from LinkedIn cold outreach.
- A platform change within outbound is more similar to existing skills than changing
  methods from outbound to paid advertising.
- Alex says LinkedIn outbound can scale beyond 100 requests through alternative systems
  and platforms, without specifying tactics in this excerpt.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Price is the strongest lever on profit."

> "Changing methods is the hardest thing to do."

> "You should keep doing outbound."
