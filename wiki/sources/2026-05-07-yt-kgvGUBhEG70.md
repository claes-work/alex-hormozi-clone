# How to Turn Cold Traffic Into Buyers

## Summary

Alex coaches a poker-coaching business whose warm funnel works but cold traffic breaks
even or worse. He says cold traffic is being put through a warm sales motion and needs
to be "microwaved" through a paid workshop, qualification data, and a stronger
pre-call value experience.

## Key claims (Alex's advice, dated 2026-05-07)

- Alex says warm traffic and cold traffic often require different sales motions.
- He says cold traffic going through a warm motion will not convert well.
- He says a cold motion can also make warm traffic more sold and potentially support a
  higher price.
- He recommends a paid workshop, likely $49 to $99, rather than a free webinar.
- He says a small purchase filters unqualified people and makes the next purchase more
  likely.
- He recommends delivering real value for roughly three hours and selling at the end.
- He recommends stacking the $99 offer so it feels absurdly valuable.
- He recommends collecting qualification data, especially the player's average buy-in,
  and optimizing ad data around qualified buyers rather than raw leads.
- He says qualified conversion rate matters more than overall webinar conversion rate.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "We need to microwave your cold traffic to make it warm."

> "Buyers buy more."

> "Juice the hell out of that $99 offer."
