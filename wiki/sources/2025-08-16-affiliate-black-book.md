# Affiliate Black Book — $100M Money Models Launch

**What it is.** An 18-page internal handbook Acquisition.com distributed to affiliates
ahead of the *$100M Money Models* free virtual book launch (Saturday, 2025-08-16, 9am
PT, live on YouTube). It contains ready-to-send promotional copy for every channel plus
the affiliate incentive structure. Raw file:
[raw/2025-08-16-affiliate-black-book.md](../../raw/2025-08-16-affiliate-black-book.md)

**Attribution note.** Most of the document is swipe copy written FOR affiliates to send

## Summary

The handbook packages one launch campaign as a complete affiliate kit:

1. **Event mechanics** — free virtual launch, registration via personal affiliate
   "magic link", attendance gated live-only (PDF p. 2).
2. **Meta ad copy arsenal** — 6 ad variants, each a different persuasion angle: curiosity
   ("secret project"), problem/solution, social proof ("over 1 million registered"),
   urgency ("Miss it, and you miss it forever"), direct benefit (PDF p. 4–6).
3. **Email countdown sequence** — 6 emails at 21/14/7/4/1/0 days out, each restating the
   same core promise while escalating urgency and reporting affiliate-prize progress
   (PDF p. 6–11).
4. **SMS + friend-text scripts, social group posts, Slack messages, organic posts** —
   the same message re-voiced per channel intimacy level (PDF p. 12–14).
5. **YouTube video script outlines** — two full 8–12-minute promo video structures with
   timestamped talking-point beats (PDF p. 15–17).
6. **Affiliate prize ladder** — 10 referrals: *70 Profit Tactics* audiobook + Vegas HQ
   drawing; top 50: guaranteed day at the Vegas HQ; top 10: 30-min fireside chat with
   Alex, live-streamed (PDF p. 7–8).
7. **Alex Hormozi Writing Guidelines** — his 8 codified writing rules (PDF p. 17–18).

## The writing guidelines (Alex-attributed method)

Verbatim rule set (PDF p. 17–18) — see [[../topics/content-strategy/hormozi-writing-guidelines|hormozi-writing-guidelines]]:

1. Keep everything below 3rd-grade reading level.
2. Present voice ("When you create your sales page", not "when you're creating").
3. Active, not passive.
4. Avoid adverbs ("adverbs usually mean lame verbs"); ban "very, super, way, actually".
5. Simple/short sentences — usually one comma; two commas → cut the middle or split.
6. Positive language ("Keep Going", not "Don't Stop").
7. Remove redundant words.
8. Remove unnecessary words ("He exited his car", not "He was able to get out of his car").
