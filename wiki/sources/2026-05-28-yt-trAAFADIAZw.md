# How to Sell a $2K+ Service in a Saturated Market

## Summary

Alex Hormozi answers a short question about selling Google Ads services to wedding photographers. His answer is direct: run Google Ads, but recognize that wedding photography has easy lead generation and poor lead quality because the market is crowded. A $2K+ service needs higher-friction sales, content, ads from winning content, and live call/webinar funnels into one-on-one sales calls.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- For a Google Ads agency selling to wedding photographers, the obvious proof move is to run Google Ads.
- Wedding photography leads are easy to generate, but lead quality can be poor because the market is saturated.
- Higher-ticket services need higher-friction sales processes.
- Content and ads have merged: make lots of content, then turn winning content into ads.
- With little traffic, send prospects to a live call; with more traffic, send them to a webinar.
- The funnel can be simple: content/ad to live call or webinar, then one-on-one sales call.
