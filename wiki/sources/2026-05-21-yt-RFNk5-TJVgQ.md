# The Painful Stage You Must Sit In to Get Rich

## Summary

Alex Hormozi describes a five-stage learning or income-pursuit cycle: uninformed optimism,
informed pessimism, the valley of despair, informed optimism, and achievement. The key
move is staying in the painful third stage long enough to learn the variables instead of
jumping to a new opportunity and restarting the same loop.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- New entrepreneurs and income-seekers often begin with uninformed optimism.
- After entering the opportunity, they reach informed pessimism when they discover the
  downside and complexity.
- The third stage is the valley of despair, where losses, confusion, or pain peak.
- At the valley of despair, people either jump to a new opportunity and restart the loop
  or stay long enough to learn the game.
- Breaking through requires informed optimism: understanding the rules and variables even
  if the opportunity is less attractive than originally imagined.
- The fifth stage is achievement.
- Staying in the pain gives the catalyst to learn how to get out of pain.
- Pulling the parachute too early prevents the person from learning the individual skills
  and variables that affect the outcome.
- Some people repeat the same cycle across crypto, NFTs, or other new opportunities.
