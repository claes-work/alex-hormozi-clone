# The Easiest Paid Ad Strategy for Any Local Business

## Summary

Alex coaches a dentist who moved markets and needs more patients quickly. He says the
owner is advertising like it is 15 years ago and should run local social ads with simple
radius targeting, opt-in forms, scheduling, and no-show card capture. For a more
aggressive push, he recommends a free Invisalign giveaway with second-place discount
follow-up.

## Key claims

- Alex says the dentist's existing mailers, Yelp, and Google mix is outdated relative to
  current social advertising.
- He says local ads are easier because targeting can be a simple radius around the
  office.
- He recommends before/after-style content, local Meta ads, opt-in forms, and front-desk
  follow-up.
- He recommends collecting a card for a no-show fee because cold traffic produces
  no-shows.
- He says the script for card collection can simply be that this is how the office has
  always done it.
- He recommends a free Invisalign giveaway, countdown messages, one winner, and calling
  everyone else as a second-place discount lead.
