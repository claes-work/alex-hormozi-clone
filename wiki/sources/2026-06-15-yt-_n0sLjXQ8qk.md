# Helping a Pilot Scale His Niche Organic Ads Business

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises a mortgage broker focused on airline and military pilots using VA loans. Alex compares channels by actual fit, not just aggregate CAC, and says generic realtor referrals are weak because most realtors do not regularly serve pilots. He recommends doubling down on pilot-specific organic content, community, lead magnets, and targeted ads, because interest-media algorithms can identify pilots if the content is genuinely pilot-specific.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - the coach. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible.
- **Steve / coachee** - airline/military pilot mortgage broker. His revenue, profit, workshop attendance, deal sources, LTV/CAC estimates, and finance-course lead magnet are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Channel analysis should separate sources rather than using only blended CAC.
- Generic realtors are a poor affiliate channel for a highly niched pilot mortgage offer because few of their clients are pilots.
- The better outbound target is group/community owners or businesses that already aggregate pilots.
- A free finance course can become the lead magnet for organic and paid acquisition.
- A pilots-only community can nurture not-ready buyers and generate referrals.
- Paid ads can be highly targeted for this avatar because platforms understand interest graphs.
- The platform environment is interest media, not social media: to reach pilots, make pilot-specific content.
- Winning organic posts can be repurposed into ads by adding a CTA and sending traffic to the lead magnet/community.
- Pilot-related humor and inside jokes can work as top-of-funnel content for this niche.
