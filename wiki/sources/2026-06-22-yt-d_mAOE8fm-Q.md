# $4M Gym Business: What's Stopping Me From Hitting $10M?

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises the same gym-growth-platform founder as the related 2026-06-22 upload. The advice is materially the same: use metrics to understand the current ceiling, build fulfillment capacity before increasing demand, and recognize that Alex's own Gym Launch exit was partly driven by losing love for the avatar and not seeing the larger opportunity at the time.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- The current sales velocity and customer value imply only limited remaining growth if nothing changes.
- The business should scale CAC and demand only after enough fulfillment capacity exists.
- If capacity is constrained, increasing demand may protect short-term profit but damage reputation and metrics.
- The founder's gym-owner avatar fatigue prompts Alex to explain his own Gym Launch exit.
- Alex says the trigger is no longer being in love with the avatar.
- Alex says he became resentful and did not see how big Gym Launch could become.
- He now believes he could have built Gym Launch into a much larger exit with current knowledge.
