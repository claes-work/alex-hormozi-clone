# This One 2-Minute Video Made Me $10 Million

## Summary

Alex says he regrets not documenting more of his failures and low points. He recalls
launching more than 30 gyms but making only one short recording, which later became an
ad that made him roughly $10 million. He frames documentation as a local cost with a
global benefit: it costs effort now, especially during low points, but later creates
memory, story, proof, and marketing assets.
