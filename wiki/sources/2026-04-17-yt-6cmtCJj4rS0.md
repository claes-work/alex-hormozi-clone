# Outwork Your Self-Doubt

## Summary

Alex restates his confidence thesis: confidence is built by stacking proof, not by
affirmations. When someone has no big wins yet, the evidence starts with tiny impulses and
small completed actions. Massive dreams begin with conquering small impulses repeatedly.

## Key claims

- On 2026-04-17, Alex says the first step to a massive dream is conquering tiny impulses.
- He restates that confidence does not come from shouting affirmations but from building
  an undeniable stack of proof.
- He says beginners overlook the smaller evidence they already have, such as getting
  dressed, showing up, or sitting in front of the computer.
- He says large outcomes come from many constrained tiny impulses and small victories.
- He frames the loop as accumulating evidence that the person has done what they say they
  can do.
