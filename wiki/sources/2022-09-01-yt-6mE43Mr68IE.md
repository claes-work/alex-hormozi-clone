## Key claims (attributed)

All claims below are **Kale Owen's / Gym Launch's** (framework credited to Alex Hormozi):
- The CLOSER formula lets you write any sales script without a rigid script: Clarify why they came, Label their problem, Overview past pain/experiences, Sell the vacation (the outcome, not the work), Explain away concerns, Reinforce the decision.
- The three primary objections are always stalling, money, or not being the decision maker ("I need more time / I don't have enough money / I need to talk to my spouse").
- "Sell the vacation, not the plane ride" — paint the end result, not the effort required.
- Reinforce immediately after sale (swag, handwritten note, welcome video) to boost retention and referrals.
- Gym Launch scaled to "over 100 million dollars" and clients "sold over 500 million dollars worth of fitness contracts" using this formula.
