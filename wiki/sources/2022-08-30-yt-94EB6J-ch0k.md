## Key claims (attributed)

All claims below are **Kale Owen's / Gym Launch's** (one framework credited to Alex), not spoken by Alex:
- Eight-part irresistible-offer checklist: (1) pre-frame the prospect's mindset with social proof/authority, (2) price anchoring against a higher number, (3) splinter stack (break the offer into itemized bonuses to inflate perceived value), (4) scarcity (limited spots), (5) urgency (deadline), (6) crazy guarantee (reverses risk; forces you to deliver), (7) free ethical bribe with a dollar value attached, (8) "down-sell your upsell."
- **Down-sell-upsell:** move a prospect from a high-ticket 6-week front-end ($599, ~$100/week) into a 12-month agreement by lowering weekly price while increasing length of commitment — "downsell weekly price, upsell length of time." Kale credits this to Alex Hormozi.
- Price anchoring works even when the prospect knows they're being anchored.
- Gym Launch claims to have been "probably the only people in the world" teaching the down-sell-upsell, which "Alex was the one that kind of created."
