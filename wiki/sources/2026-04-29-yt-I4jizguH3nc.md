# You Can't Be Busy and Broke

## Summary

Alex answers a founder who wants a bigger brand without ad budget or time for content. He
says zero inputs cannot produce a large output, so the realistic low-cash path is
outreach to people who already have the desired audience. The founder should identify
mega-influencers serving young aspiring entrepreneurs, warm them up, provide custom value
in advance, and borrow their existing distribution.

## Key claims

- Alex rejects the premise of building a big brand with neither money nor time.
- If a person is busy and still broke, Alex says they are doing the wrong work.
- With no ad budget, the main available input is outreach.
- Outreach can leverage someone else's audience, time, and money.
- Warming up an influencer can take months and should include genuine value, custom work,
  tags, mentions, and appreciation.
- Alex says this path can be faster than building a brand alone, but it still takes time.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "You can't be both busy and broke."

> "I have no inputs, but I would like tremendous output."

> "Demonstrate value in advance."
