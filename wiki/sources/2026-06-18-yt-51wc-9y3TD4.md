# Helping a Pilot Scale His Aviation Safety Business

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi pushes an aviation safety training founder to clarify the money goal and constraint. The caller describes several possible markets, including wealthy helicopter owners, insurance underwriters, and broke pilots. Alex repeatedly narrows the issue to the current working offer: the founder makes about $500,000 a year but is supply constrained by one-on-one delivery. Alex's final recommendation is to change the delivery ratio into a group or more scalable model and then advertise more.

- The fix is to change the delivery ratio, such as group delivery, then advertise more.
