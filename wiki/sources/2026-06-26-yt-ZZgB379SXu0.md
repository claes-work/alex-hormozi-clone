# Helping a Fitness Coach Double His Business in 6 Months

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises an online fitness coach doing about $600K revenue and $300K profit who wants to double within six months. The founder thinks about doubling lead flow through ads, but Alex focuses on content leverage, reposting top trial Reels to new audiences, delegating content research/scripting, and hiring sales capacity before turning up lead flow.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - the coach. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible.
- **Joseph** - online health and fitness coach. His revenue, profit, audience, lead magnet, DM flow, and sales-call load are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- If a creator already posts daily and has a working organic engine, the next move may be better content or more leverage, not simply double the output.
- Alex suggests delegating or automating research and scripting because many large creators do not do all of their own content research or scripting.
- A personal brand can be systematized: the founder can write down the content system and teach others to help execute it.
- Trial Reels can be used aggressively because they reach new audiences; Alex suggests reposting the top 20-30 Reels from the last year every month.
- Organic content prospects are warmer than cold ad leads, so straight-to-DM ads may underperform unless a true cold conversion mechanism exists.
- Before increasing lead flow, the founder should create sales capacity; otherwise more leads either go uncaught or pull the founder away from content.
- Alex does not recommend turning on ads while the founder is still the salesperson.
