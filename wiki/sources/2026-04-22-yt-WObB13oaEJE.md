# Don't Sacrifice Your Brand for Views

## Summary

Alex says many content creators damage their brand by chasing whatever the algorithm
currently rewards. He says his team could clip old vlog footage to make him look bad
and get large views, but that would not help his long-term goals. His advice is to stay
on brand, talk about what one actually knows, and avoid pretending to be someone else.

## Key claims

- Alex says his post-Gym Launch epiphany was that he had not understood brand well
  enough during Gym Launch.
- He says content teams can manufacture high-view moments that damage the brand.
- He criticizes creators who will do anything for the algorithm and chase hot formats
  outside their expertise.
- He says if a topic is not on brand or not within his expertise, he will not talk about
  it.
- He advises creators to talk about what they actually know rather than impersonating a
  broader authority.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "They have to understand brand."

> "If it's not on brand for me... I'm not going to talk about it."

> "Just talk about stuff you know about."
