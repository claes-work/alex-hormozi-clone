# Helping a $100K Construction Business Get to $3M

## Summary

Alex coaches a residential construction business doing about $100K/year that wants to get
to $3M. The founders are considering niching into bathrooms. Alex likes the idea because
bathrooms are high-margin, fast-turnaround, easier to advertise with specific before/after
proof, and more straightforward to quote and close. He advises keeping current revenue
coming in while shifting marketing focus until the mix changes.

## Key claims

- On 2026-04-17, Alex says niching a construction business into a specialty it can "nail"
  can make advertising much more responsive.
- He likes the bathroom niche because the founders report roughly 42% margins, a two-week
  turnaround, and potentially more capacity.
- He says small businesses normally accept money from anyone at first; the shift should
  be in the advertising focus before turning off broader work.
- He recommends focusing ads on the faster, more specific bathroom offer while still
  taking other jobs until the money mix changes.
- He says over time the "coloring" of the revenue can shift until the business can say no
  to the unfocused work.
