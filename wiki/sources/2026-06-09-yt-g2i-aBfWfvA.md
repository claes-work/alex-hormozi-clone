# $300K/Month With 80% Margins on Instagram Organic

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises an Argentina-based consulting business doing about $300K/month top line and $240K/month bottom line from Instagram organic. The owner gets 20,000-30,000 leads per month but only about 80 clients. Alex rejects lowering ticket price, says the business is already highly profitable, and identifies the likely bottleneck as DM setters: 700 leads/day producing only about 20 booked calls means the leads are not being worked. He recommends more DM setters and lead nurture before changing offer price.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Do not lower ticket price when the business is already producing high profit with a $3K-$9K offer.
- Improving show rate from 50% to 70% would materially grow the business, so lead nurture is worth improving.
- With 20,000 leads and 80 customers, conversion is roughly 0.3%; Alex guesses 1% may be possible.
- The diagnostic path is to walk through the funnel from lead to customer.
- If 700 leads/day only creates about 20 booked calls/day, the bottleneck is likely DM setter capacity.
- Show rate and qualification can improve, but they are not the main concern if the lead volume is barely being worked.
- The business should hire many more DM setters and use automation where possible.
