# Helping an Online Coaching Business Scale Past $10M

## Summary

In this coaching Q&A, Alex Hormozi advises an automated online piano-course seller whose YouTube keyword channel has plateaued. Alex says conversion optimization is the lowest-risk lever and may double the business, but paid ads to the existing audience are the most likely path from $1.4 million to $3.6 million. He explains that interest-media algorithms create a much larger warm audience than subscribers alone, so ads can reach both subscribers and lurkers who already consumed the content and therefore convert better than true cold traffic.

## Speakers & attribution

- **Alex Hormozi** - the coach. The advice below is Alex-attributed and persona-eligible.
- **Coachee** - online piano-course seller. His price point, YouTube funnel, subscriber base, prank-channel test, options, and revenue goal are context only.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Conversion-rate optimization is low risk and can potentially double a funnel, but it cannot produce unlimited growth.
- If the goal is a moderate scale-up, paid ads to an existing content audience may be the best bet.
- Subscriber and follower counts do not represent the full warm audience under interest-media algorithms.
- Many people consume a creator's content without subscribing or following.
- Ads can reach the larger pool of people who have shown interest through views, not just subscribers.
- Content creates brand awareness at functionally zero media cost, which makes the creator's funnel convert better than a cold advertiser's funnel.
- Alex recommends learning ads aggressively by buying courses, watching free material, hiring an agency, and making the agency teach the process.
- Once ads are understood, the operator has effectively bought a money-printing machine.
