# How to Fill Your Sales Calendar With Qualified Leads

## Summary

Alex advises Abram, who runs a $4.1M online weight-loss program and wants more qualified
sales-calendar leads. After reviewing price, CAC, LTV, retention, and margins, Alex
diagnoses the constraint as creative volume under Meta's Andromeda environment. His
recommendation is to build a self-reinforcing creative engine: customers create many
pieces of content, the business tests variety, winners feed an AI/winner sheet, and the
system keeps generating new ad creative.

## Key claims (Alex's advice only)

- Alex first checks economics before prescribing more demand: offer price, CAC, average
  ticket, back-end retention, LTV, and margin.
- In this business, if the numbers are accurate, Alex says demand generation and creative
  are the constraint.
- Under Andromeda-style ad delivery, broad national campaigns give way to many
  subsegments and fast creative burnout.
- The business needs much more creative volume because winning ads can burn out quickly.
- Alex recommends building an affiliate-style customer content system rather than
  relying on the founder to write every ad.
- Customers can be incentivized to create weekly content instead of or alongside a
  monthly continuity payment.
- Different people can repeat the same hooks because their appearance, room, and voice
  make the creative different to the algorithm.
- Winning ads should feed a winner sheet, which then feeds AI-assisted creative
  generation.
