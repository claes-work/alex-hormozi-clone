# THE SKOOL GAMES: The Easiest Business to start right now

## Summary

A solo Alex Hormozi promotional monologue pitching the Skool Games and starting a paid community on Skool as, in his framing, the easiest business to start today. He argues it requires no startup capital, no inventory, no employees, and no pre-existing audience or expertise, and lays out the prizes (a day in Vegas with him and 10 top performers), stated platform success statistics, and a risk-free 14-day free-trial offer. The video functions as a direct-response sales pitch driving viewers to sign up for a free Skool trial and join the Skool Games tournament.

- **Alex Hormozi** — sole speaker throughout. This is a single-narrator scripted promotional video; there is no interview or second voice. All content is Alex-attributed.

(paraphrase)
