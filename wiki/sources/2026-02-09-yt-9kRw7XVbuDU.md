# I Do $10M/Year Building Luxury Homes. How Do I Attract A Players?

## Summary
Live Q&A clip from @MoreMozi. A luxury-home builder (also named Alex) from Texas explains he builds ~six homes a year at ~$1.5M each ($10M top line, ~$2M net), does all the sales himself, and wants to scale to $30M by finding one top A-player salesperson so he can get his time back. Alex Hormozi reframes the problem: every business is demand-constrained — either for customers or for talent — and both are solved with advertising/outreach; you recruit A-players the same way you find customers. For a sales leader/top producer, the primary channel is outreach, specifically LinkedIn. He advises expanding the search region and offering relocation packages, and diagnoses the questioner's failed attempt (a formal letter to ~15 local top producers) as insufficient volume: add "two zeros" to the effort. Clip ends with a promo for his free acquisition.com 10-stage $0–$100M+ roadmap.
