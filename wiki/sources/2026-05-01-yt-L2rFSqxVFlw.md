# How to Fix a Dead Community

## Summary

Alex advises a small Australian business-networking community to revive engagement by
doing unscalable matchmaking. The operator should DM members, learn their businesses,
connect people who can help each other, and let those members associate the value with
the group. He also recommends picture-based prompts that bring members' real-world
context into the community.

## Key claims

- For a small dead community, Alex recommends manual DM work and matchmaking before
  scalable tactics.
- Community members will associate valuable introductions with the person and group that
  created the connection.
- Niche-specific picture prompts can stimulate participation by moving members' real
  environments into the group.
- Examples include asking manufacturers to show their rig, home-gym owners to show their
  gym, designers to show their desk, or musicians to show their setup.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Try play matchmaker."

> "This is unscalable and that's okay."

> "Take the real world and put it inside of your community."
