# I Challenged My Team To Replace Themselves with AI (2023-02-08)

## Summary

An early (Feb 2023) solo showcase in which Hormozi says he told his entire
acquisition.com team to "replace themselves with AI in a week," then walks through the
nine tools they came back with across sales, marketing/content, training/onboarding,
recruiting, and operations. The framing is almost entirely **tactical and
tool-by-tool** — time saved per task, output per worker — rather than the strategic
"AI reshapes the firm" thesis he would develop by 2026. He closes with a joke (then a
prediction) about robots building his holdco headquarters.

## Key claims (his Feb-2023 AI position)

- He challenged his whole team to "replace themselves with AI" within a week; they
  returned with nine tools spanning sales, content, training, recruiting, and ops.
- **Sales:** personalized AI-lipsynced videos of himself to lift meeting show-up rates
  (records one clip; AI swaps his mouth to say names he never spoke); compose.ai for
  email follow-ups, cutting ~2 hrs/day of writing. He frames the ~2 hrs saved on a
  40-hour week as "25% more output for the same cost."
- **Content (250 pieces/week):** resemble.ai voice cloning for audio versions of his
  tweets/emails and real-time voice memos ("freakishly like me... only ~10 minutes of
  training"); runway.ai for fast image editing / object & background removal;
  descript for transcript-based editing so editors skip manual clip-hunting and spend
  the time on packaging/emotion.
- He predicts AI voice cloning will let **audiobooks be recorded entirely by AI** and
  every blog post get an audio version; cites Podcast.ai scraping Joe Rogan's voice as
  proof of where it's headed.
- **Training/onboarding:** AI avatar presenters ("Jessica, an AI generated member of
  the acquisition.com team") for onboarding videos as a "living document" — edit text,
  video updates instantly — eliminating "hundreds of hours of human labor."
- Productivity framing: the two things that raise productivity per worker are
  **education and technology**, and AI-based training uses both at once.
- **Recruiting:** Tavus for personalized candidate outreach video; Otter.ai for
  interview notes/timestamps (saves ~10–15 min per call).
- **Operations:** Fireflies.ai to attend meetings and take notes; ChatGPT to
  consolidate them; reclaim.ai to reshuffle team calendars and cut dead white-space
  between meetings, claimed to boost company output by "40%."
- Closing prediction: robots/AI will help build their new holdco HQ — "work around the
  clock, not need time off... no fear of them getting injured." Calls the robot line a
  joke "but in the not too far future it won't be."

## Notes

- **Alex is the sole speaker** and main subject; every line above is Alex-attributed.
  Non-Alex audio elements are clearly synthetic and not persona data: the AI "Bro Jogan
  Experience" clip (AI-generated Rogan parody) and the AI onboarding avatar "Jessica"
  are tool demos, not Hormozi's own words — do not train voice on them.
- **DATED early-2023 AI take — flag the evolution.** This is a snapshot of Hormozi's
  *earliest public* AI stance, ~3 years before the thesis in
  [[../topics/business/ai-in-business]] (spring 2026). Compare:
  - **Continuity (seeds of the 2026 view):** the "replace yourself / automate your own
    job" instinct is already here — in 2026 it becomes "if you're not automating your
    own job, you are missing the boat." The **workflow/task decomposition** reflex
    (measuring each role's tasks in hours saved) prefigures 2026's "workflow-based, not
    role-based thinking." And "capturing" his tweets/voice as audio content anticipates
    the 2026 **"capture, don't create"** doctrine, though here it's a tooling convenience,
    not yet a proof-generation strategy.
  - **Evolution / what's missing in 2023:** this video is a **tool parade** — the value
    prop is time saved and output multiplied per task. It contains none of the 2026
    strategic frame: no "use AI as a tool, don't become an AI company" (tool-not-identity),
    no risk/proof/credibility moat, no "humans-plus-tools beat humans-with-inferior-tools,"
    no barbell strategy, no margin-leverage argument. The 2023 Hormozi is enthusiastically
    tactical and even a bit techno-utopian ("hundreds of hours... eliminated," robots
    building HQ); the 2026 Hormozi frames AI as "a necessary evil" and centers the
    *durable human edge* (risk, proof, judgment). Present this only as his **2023**
    position; do not let its tone stand in for his current view.
- Tools named (for reference, tactical only): AI lipsync video, compose.ai, resemble.ai,
  runway.ai, descript, Tavus, Otter.ai, Fireflies.ai, reclaim.ai, ChatGPT, Podcast.ai.
- Quantitative claims (25% more output, 40% calendar gain, ~10–15 min/call saved,
  250 pieces/week) are **self-reported** productivity figures from his own team, not
  independently verified.
